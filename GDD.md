# PIZSTEIN ISLAND — Game Design Document v0.2

## Logline
A lewd, crude, and occasionally transcendent puzzle-platformer. A princess. A cursed island run by sentient pizzas. A goddess who never misses. Let's fn go.

## Protagonists

### Throother (Player Character)
- Princess, lost, ends up on Pizstein Island
- Starts with basic physical abilities
- Full power unlocked: **The Artemis Arrow** (see Powers)

### Artemis — Lady of Hope (Guide)
- Goddess guide, appears throughout
- Her power is retrocausal: the victim sees the arrow already in their body, looks up, sees Artemis *just about to nock the arrow*. She never misses. She is Artemis.
- Appears in cutscenes and as a voice/presence when Throother needs guidance

## The Pizzas

### Good Pizzas
- Willingly sacrifice themselves to Throother — manna-like (Numbers 11:9 energy, not overtly religious, pure satire)
- Nourishing, restorative, earnest
- Gerald is the nicest pizza on the island

### Bad Pizzas
- Predatory, dangerous
- Roll around leaving **cheese grease trails** (patrol tells, environmental hazard, faction identifier)
  - Fresh trail = Cheese pizza nearby, slippery
  - Congealed trail = Pepperoni zone, slightly hardened, flammable in Inverse State
  - Crystallized trail = Grande Margherita territory — practically a road at this point
  - Kombucha trail = Vegan faction. Slightly sticky. Smells like a co-op.
- On encounter: sprout arms and legs, transform into **pizza-faced rich guys in suits**
- The face is the pizza. The pizza is the face. The metaphor is the point.

## The Daily Event: THE SHARK FEED
- Once a day, the island elites gather on the yacht
- The nicest pizza (Gerald) gets ceremonially fed to the sharks
- Rich guys in linen pants applaud from the shore
- **Throother's mission each day: save Gerald**
- The only way to reach the yacht in time: **Coin State phasing**

## The Three Reality States

### Physical State
Normal gameplay. Touch, fight, eat, run. Time moves. Space is fixed.

### Inverse State
Flipped physics. Gravity reverses, locked doors open, enemy vulnerabilities flip.
- Grease trails: congealed Pepperoni trails become flammable

### Coin State (S-State)
Phase out of physical existence. No time. No space. Navigate via **fold geometry**.
- You must learn the geometry to steer — drift = lost forever
- Based on 1729 = 7x13x19 structural logic (Ramanujan fold geometry)
- Puzzles teach: physical A→B is linear. Inverse A→B is reflected. Coin state: A and B are the same point if you know the fold.
- Required to reach the yacht for the daily Shark Feed rescue

## Enemy Codex

### FACTION 1: THE CRUSTED ELITE (Suit Pizzas)
*They have money. They have power. They have pizza face. They are the problem.*

#### Cheese Pizza Guy — Tier 1
- Entry-level rich guy. Has money, no taste.
- 37" of dangling, drippy mozzarella hangs from his belt like a weapon he didn't choose but fully commits to
- Estonian wife referenced but never seen
- **Special Attack — The Zit Pop**: scab-covered mozz bubbles on his face erupt, launching pepperoni shrapnel. Disgusting. Effective.
- **Weakness**: Slow, predictable, deeply insecure about being "just cheese"
- **Defeat animation**: Deflates. The mozz goes limp. He mutters something about his net worth.

#### Pepperoni Guy — Tier 2
- Fancies himself a serious player. More aggressive, harder to stagger. Smells worse.
- The Stick: unsliced pepperoni rod. He is very proud of it. You will have to deal with it.
- Grease trail: congealed, flammable in Inverse State

#### Margherita (The Traitor) — Tier 3 / Mini-Boss
- Female variant. The Enabler. She didn't have to be here. She chose this.
- Polished exterior, rotten underneath. Talks nice, fights dirty.
- **Special — The Press Conference**: Stuns with a speech that sounds reasonable until it doesn't. Applies Confusion debuff.
- Inspiration: you know who. They all know who.

#### Grande Margherita — BOSS
- The Big Fat One. Big energy. Enormous presence. Takes forever to kill — not because she's hard to damage but because she is A Lot.
- Health pool: technically not that high
- **Attack pattern**: Mostly screaming. Occasional sit. The shockwave from the sit is real.
- **Special — The Rosanne Riff**: Says unhinged things dealing psychic damage over time. You don't block it. You endure it.
- **Victory condition**: Outlast her. That's the puzzle. That's the whole puzzle.
- **Defeat line**: "I was just doing a bit."

### FACTION 2: THE CRUST INTELLIGENTSIA (Vegan Pizzas)
*They think they're better than the other pizzas. They are not. Kombucha grease trails.*

> NOTE: The Suit faction and the Vegan faction appear opposed. They are not. This is a Level 3 revelation.

#### Vegan Cheese Pizza — Woody Allen Type
- Sweaty. Glasses. Mumbling. Perpetually explaining why what you're doing to him is actually more harmful to you.
- **Special — The Monologue**: Launches into an unprompted 4-minute neurotic spiral. No damage, but uninterruptible. You just stand there.
- **Weakness**: Direct eye contact. He flinches.
- **Defeat line**: "I feel like this says more about YOU than it does about me."

#### Vegan Fried Egg Pizza — Noam Chomsky Type
- Deeply disappointed in you specifically.
- **Special — The Lecture**: Damages INT stat temporarily. You get dumber just from listening.
- **Weakness**: Ask one follow-up question. He answers for 45 minutes and forgets to fight you.
- **Defeat line**: "I've been saying this since 1967."

#### Vegan Veggie Pizza — The Vegetable
*(no vegetable joke intended. yes vegetable joke intended. satire is funny.)*
- Toppings arranged in a specific way. Small motorized cart involved.
- **Special — Theoretical Framework**: Immaculate in concept. Completely stationary in execution.
- **Movement speed**: 0
- **Threat level**: Surprisingly high because you feel bad the whole time
- **Flavor text**: "The question is not whether you can fight me. The question is whether fighting me is even the right frame."

#### Stephen Hawking Pizza — BOSS (The Big Brain Pie)
- Full motorized pizza wheelchair. Toppings arranged into a face of pure cosmic condescension.
- Voice: synthesized. Every line same flat affect regardless of content. His trash talk is devastating.
- HP: Enormous — not because he's tough but because you keep stopping to listen
- **Phase 1 — Event Horizon**: Creates a gravity well you must navigate around
- **Phase 2 — The Singularity**: Collapses into Coin State. Chase him through fold geometry. He is weirdly good at it.
- **Special — Brief History of Your Failure**: Narrates everything you did wrong in the fight, in order, in that voice.
- **Defeat line**: "........." (long pause) "...Remarkable."

## Boss: TRUMPLETHINPEN
- Lives under the bridge
- Distinctive blonde swoof of hair. He is a troll.
- Signs "Executive Orders" commanding the evil pizzas
- Orders are absurdist decrees ("All good pizzas must register their toppings")
- Weak point: his pen. His oddly thin, oddly small pen.
- Defeat condition: get him to sign an order that backfires on himself

## Powers
- **Artemis Arrow**: When fully powered, Throother's finishing move. Cutscene: enemy sees the arrow already in their body. Looks up. Artemis is just about to nock. It's already over.

## Tone
Lewd, crude, satirical, occasionally profound. South Park level — everyone gets it, no one is safe, the absurdity is the point. The geometry puzzles smuggle in real ideas about physical vs non-physical reality. The manna pizzas smuggle in something ancient. The troll under the bridge is unmistakable. The vegetable joke was always intended.

The game is ridiculous. Ridiculousness is the design doc.
